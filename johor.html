Bak Kut Teh 肉骨茶 ⭐⭐⭐⭐☆ (4.6/5)

Bak Kut Teh is a beloved Johor dish featuring pork ribs simmered in a fragrant broth of Chinese herbs, garlic, soy sauce, and star anise, offering a rich and comforting flavor popular in Johor’s food scene.

Normal Price: RM 12.00 - RM 18.00

Popular in: Johor Bahru, Malaysia

Best Served With: Rice & You Tiao

More Info

Ingredients:

  • Pork ribs
  • Chinese herbs
  • Garlic
  • Soy sauce
  • Star anise

Kway Chap 粿汁 ⭐⭐⭐⭐☆ (4.5/5)

Kway Chap is a Teochew specialty featuring flat rice noodles in a savory soy-braised broth, served with tender pork belly, tofu, boiled eggs, and offal, a favorite in Johor Bahru’s hawker stalls.

Normal Price: RM 8.00 - RM 15.00

Popular in: Johor Bahru, Malaysia

Best Served With: Braised Pork Belly & Eggs

More Info

Ingredients:

  • Flat rice noodles
  • Soy-braised broth
  • Pork belly
  • Tofu
  • Boiled egg

Lei Cha 擂茶 ⭐⭐⭐⭐☆ (4.4/5)

Lei Cha, or Thunder Tea Rice, is a Hakka dish featuring rice topped with a variety of vegetables, peanuts, and tofu, served with a unique green herbal tea soup, celebrated in Johor’s culinary culture.

Normal Price: RM 6.00 - RM 10.00

Popular in: Johor, Malaysia

Best Served With: Pickled Vegetables

More Info

Ingredients:

  • Rice
  • Vegetables
  • Peanuts
  • Green tea soup
  • Tofu
🔍

Mee Rebus 面煮 ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (4.8/5)

Mee Rebus is a flavorful noodle dish with yellow noodles smothered in a thick, savory gravy made from sweet potatoes, curry powder, dried shrimp, and peanuts, garnished with a hard-boiled egg in Johor’s street food stalls.

Normal Price: RM 5.00 - RM 8.00

Popular in: Johor, Malaysia

Best Served With: Lime & Green Chili

More Info

Ingredients:

  • Yellow noodles
  • Sweet potato-based gravy
  • Dried shrimp
  • Peanuts
  • Hard-boiled egg
🔍

Otak-Otak 乌达 ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (4.7/5)

Otak-Otak is a fragrant grilled fish cake made from ground fish meat blended with coconut milk, chili paste, and turmeric, wrapped in banana leaves and grilled to perfection, a specialty in Muar, Johor.

Normal Price: RM 1.50 - RM 3.00 per piece

Popular in: Muar, Johor

Best Served With: Rice & As a Snack

More Info

Ingredients:

  • Fish paste
  • Coconut milk
  • Chili paste
  • Turmeric
  • Banana leaves
