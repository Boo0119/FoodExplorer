Hinava 生鱼沙拉 ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (4.8/5)

Hinava is a traditional Kadazan-Dusun raw fish salad marinated with lime juice, ginger, onions, chili, and bitter gourd. It is often served during festive occasions and is a symbol of Sabah’s rich indigenous heritage.

Normal Price: RM 15.00 - RM 25.00

Popular in: Kadazan-Dusun communities

Best Served With: Steamed rice

More Info

Ingredients:

  • Raw mackerel
  • Lime juice
  • Ginger
  • Onion
  • Chili & bitter gourd

Ngiu Chap 牛杂面 ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (4.9/5)

Ngiu Chap is a hearty beef noodle soup consisting of beef slices, beef balls, and assorted offal simmered in a rich herbal broth. A comfort food for Sabahans.

Normal Price: RM 8.00 - RM 15.00

Popular in: Kota Kinabalu

Best Served With: Chili sauce

More Info

Ingredients:

  • Beef slices & offal
  • Beef balls
  • Chinese herbs
  • Rice noodles
  • Soy sauce & chili

Tuaran Mee 斗亚兰炒面 ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (4.9/5)

Tuaran Mee is Sabah’s signature noodle dish from the town of Tuaran. It is made from homemade egg noodles, stir-fried until slightly crispy, and topped with char siu, vegetables, and egg rolls.

Normal Price: RM 6.00 - RM 10.00

Popular in: Tuaran, Kota Kinabalu

Best Served With: Pickled chili

More Info

Ingredients:

  • Egg noodles
  • Char siu (barbecue pork)
  • Egg rolls
  • Vegetables
  • Soy sauce
