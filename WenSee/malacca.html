Lok Lok 乐乐 ⭐⭐⭐⭐☆ (4.5/5)

Lok Lok is a communal dining experience where skewers of meat, seafood, and vegetables are cooked in boiling broth and dipped into a variety of sauces.

Normal Price: RM 1.00 - RM 5.00 per stick

Popular in: Malacca, Malaysia

Best Served With: Satay Sauce / Chili Sauce

More Info

Ingredients:

  • Seafood skewers
  • Meat skewers
  • Vegetables
  • Broth for boiling
  • Dipping sauces
🔍

Chicken Rice Balls 鸡饭团 ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (4.8/5)

A unique Malaccan twist on Hainanese chicken rice, where the rice is molded into balls and served with tender poached chicken and flavorful sauces.

Normal Price: RM 8.00 - RM 15.00

Popular in: Malacca, Malaysia

Best Served With: Ginger sauce / Dark soy sauce

More Info

Ingredients:

  • Poached chicken
  • Rice balls
  • Ginger sauce
  • Dark soy sauce
  • Cucumber slices
🔍

Nyonya Laksa 娘惹叻沙 ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (4.7/5)

A spicy noodle soup that represents the fusion of Chinese and Malay culinary traditions, featuring thick rice noodles in a rich coconut curry broth.

Normal Price: RM 6.00 - RM 12.00

Popular in: Malacca, Penang, Singapore

Best Served With: Sambal belacan / Lime

More Info

Ingredients:

  • Rice noodles
  • Coconut milk
  • Prawns
  • Fish cake
  • Bean sprouts
  • Spice paste
🔍

Satay Celup 沙爹浸 ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (4.6/5)

A unique Malaccan dish similar to lok lok, where skewered ingredients are dipped into a pot of boiling satay sauce. It's like steamboat but with the rich, nutty flavor of peanut sauce.

Normal Price: RM 0.80 - RM 3.00 per stick

Popular in: Malacca, Malaysia

Best Served With: Ketupat / Rice cubes

More Info

Ingredients:

  • Various skewered items
  • Peanut satay sauce
  • Fish balls
  • Tofu
  • Vegetables
  • Seafood
