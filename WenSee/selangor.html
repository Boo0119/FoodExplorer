Apom Manis 甜薄饼 ⭐⭐⭐⭐☆ (4.3/5)

Apom Manis is a thin and crispy pancake, slightly sweet, and often eaten as a light snack in Malaysia.

Normal Price: RM 0.50 - RM 1.00 per piece

Popular in: Selangor, Malaysia

Best Served With: Tea or Coffee

More Info

Ingredients:

  • Rice flour
  • Coconut milk
  • Sugar
  • Yeast
  • Water

Claypot Chicken Rice is a flavorful rice dish cooked with marinated chicken, Chinese sausages, and soy sauce in a claypot.

Normal Price: RM 8.00 - RM 15.00

Popular in: Selangor, Malaysia

Best Served With: Salted fish

More Info

Ingredients:

  • Rice
  • Chicken

  • Soy sauce

Ramly Burger 兰利汉堡 ⭐⭐⭐⭐☆ (4.7/5)

The Ramly Burger is a popular Malaysian street burger, often wrapped with an egg omelette and filled with sauces.

Normal Price: RM 4.00 - RM 8.00

Popular in: Selangor, Malaysia

Best Served With: Chili sauce and mayonnaise

Satay 沙爹 ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (4.8/5)

Satay consists of skewered and grilled meat, usually served with peanut sauce, rice cakes, and cucumber.

Normal Price: RM 1.00 - RM 2.00 per stick

Popular in: Selangor, Malaysia

Best Served With: Peanut sauce and ketupat

More Info

Ingredients:

  • Chicken / Beef / Lamb
  • Bamboo skewers
  • Spices & herbs
  • Peanut sauce

Yong Tau Foo 酿豆腐 ⭐⭐⭐⭐☆ (4.4/5)

Yong Tau Foo is a dish of tofu and vegetables stuffed with fish paste, served in soup or deep-fried with sauce.

Normal Price: RM 1.00 - RM 3.00 per piece

Popular in: Selangor, Malaysia

Best Served With: Chili sauce or sweet bean sauce

More Info

Ingredients:

  • Tofu
  • Fish paste
  • Vegetables
  • Soy sauce
  • Soup stock
