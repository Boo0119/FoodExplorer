Ayam Percik ⭐⭐⭐⭐☆ (4.7/5)

Ayam Percik is grilled chicken marinated in spices and coconut milk, then basted with a rich, spicy sauce.

Normal Price: RM 5.00 - RM 10.00 per piece

Popular in: Kelantan, Malaysia

Best Served With: Nasi putih or nasi kerabu

Ingredients:

  • Chicken
  • Coconut milk
  • Chili paste

  • Spices
