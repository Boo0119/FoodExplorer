Kek Lapis 砂拉越千层蛋糕 ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (4.9/5)

Kek Lapis Sarawak is a colorful layered cake, famous for its beautiful designs and rich flavors. It is often served during celebrations and is a symbol of Sarawak hospitality.

Normal Price: RM 25.00 - RM 50.00 (per loaf)

Popular in: Kuching, Sarawak

Best Served With: Tea or coffee

More Info

Ingredients:

  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Flour
  • Condensed milk
  • Food coloring & flavorings
🔍

Kolo Mee 砂拉越干拌面 ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (4.8/5)

Kolo Mee is a beloved noodle dish of Sarawak. Served with springy noodles tossed in lard, soy sauce, and garlic oil, it’s topped with char siu, minced pork, and vegetables.

Normal Price: RM 5.00 - RM 8.00

Popular in: Kuching and Sibu

Best Served With: Pickled chilies

More Info

Ingredients:

  • Egg noodles
  • Soy sauce
  • Lard or garlic oil
  • Char siu & minced pork
  • Vegetables
🔍

Mee Jawa Rabak 爪哇面 ⭐⭐⭐⭐☆ (4.5/5)

Mee Jawa Rabak is a hearty Sarawak-style noodle dish with a sweet-spicy tomato gravy, served with boiled eggs, prawns, and fried chicken for extra flavor.

Normal Price: RM 7.00 - RM 12.00

Popular in: Kuching

Best Served With: Sambal belacan

More Info

Ingredients:

  • Yellow noodles
  • Tomato-based gravy
  • Prawns & chicken
  • Boiled eggs
  • Sambal belacan
🔍

Nasi Aruk 干炒饭 ⭐⭐⭐⭐☆ (4.4/5)

Nasi Aruk is a traditional Sarawak fried rice cooked without oil, relying instead on natural flavors from garlic, onion, and anchovies for its signature taste.

Normal Price: RM 4.00 - RM 6.00

Popular in: Rural Sarawak

Best Served With: Fried egg or sambal

More Info

Ingredients:

  • Rice
  • Anchovies
  • Garlic & onions
  • Soy sauce
  • Egg (optional)
🔍

Nasi Goreng Dabai 达拜炒饭 ⭐⭐⭐⭐☆ (4.6/5)

Nasi Goreng Dabai uses the unique dabai fruit (often called Sarawak olive) to create a creamy and savory fried rice dish distinct to Sarawak.

Normal Price: RM 6.00 - RM 10.00

Popular in: Central Sarawak

Best Served With: Fried chicken or salted fish

More Info

Ingredients:

  • Dabai fruit
  • Rice
  • Soy sauce
  • Onions & garlic
  • Salted fish or anchovies
🔍

Oyster Pancake 蚝煎 ⭐⭐⭐⭐☆ (4.5/5)

Oyster Pancake is a crispy omelette mixed with oysters, starch, and eggs, pan-fried to perfection and served with chili sauce.

Normal Price: RM 8.00 - RM 15.00

Popular in: Kuching and Sibu

Best Served With: Chili sauce

More Info

Ingredients:

  • Fresh oysters
  • Eggs
  • Potato starch
  • Garlic
  • Chili sauce
🔍

Sarawak Laksa 砂拉越叻沙 ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (4.9/5)

Sarawak Laksa is an iconic dish of Sarawak, with a unique broth made from sambal belacan, tamarind, coconut milk, and spices, topped with prawns, shredded chicken, and omelette strips.

Normal Price: RM 8.00 - RM 15.00

Popular in: Kuching

Best Served With: Sambal belacan and lime

More Info

Ingredients:

  • Rice vermicelli
  • Sambal belacan paste
  • Coconut milk
  • Prawns & chicken
  • Omelette strips
