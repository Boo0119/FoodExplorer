Kuih Karas 米花糕 ⭐⭐⭐⭐☆ (4.4/5)

Kuih Karas is a sweet and crunchy traditional snack made from rice flour and sugar, deep-fried into lace-like crisps. It is popular during festive seasons in Kedah.

Normal Price: RM 2.00 - RM 5.00 per pack

Popular in: Kedah

Best Served With: Tea or coffee

More Info

Ingredients:

  • Rice flour
  • Sugar
  • Cooking oil
🔍

Laksa Kedah 叻沙 ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (4.7/5)

Laksa Kedah is a traditional noodle dish made with rice noodles, served with a rich fish-based gravy and garnished with vegetables and herbs.

Normal Price: RM 4.00 - RM 7.00

Popular in: Kedah

Best Served With: Hard-boiled egg and sambal

More Info

Ingredients:

  • Rice noodles
  • Mackerel fish
  • Tamarind
  • Onions and garlic
  • Fresh herbs
🔍

Mee Rebus 杂锦面 ⭐⭐⭐⭐☆ (4.5/5)

Mee Rebus is a noodle dish served in a thick, slightly sweet potato-based gravy with bean sprouts, boiled eggs, and tofu.

Normal Price: RM 5.00 - RM 8.00

Popular in: Kedah & Northern Malaysia

Best Served With: Fried shallots and lime

More Info

Ingredients:

  • Yellow noodles
  • Sweet potato gravy
  • Tofu
  • Boiled egg
  • Bean sprouts
🔍

Mee Udang 虾面 ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (4.8/5)

Mee Udang is a prawn noodle dish served with a flavorful prawn-based broth, fresh prawns, and a variety of condiments.

Normal Price: RM 8.00 - RM 15.00

Popular in: Kedah (especially Kuala Sepetang)

Best Served With: Sambal and lime

More Info

Ingredients:

  • Yellow noodles
  • Prawns
  • Prawn broth
  • Egg
  • Vegetables
🔍

Nasi Lemak Royale 皇家咖喱饭 ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (4.9/5)

Nasi Lemak Royale is a famous Kedah dish where fragrant rice is served with a special yellow curry gravy, fried chicken, and spicy sambal.

Normal Price: RM 6.00 - RM 12.00

Popular in: Alor Setar, Kedah

Best Served With: Fried chicken and sambal sotong

More Info

Ingredients:

  • Coconut milk rice
  • Yellow curry gravy
  • Fried chicken
  • Sambal
  • Boiled egg
🔍

Roti Canai 印度煎饼 ⭐⭐⭐⭐☆ (4.6/5)

Roti Canai is a flaky, crispy flatbread that is pan-fried and served with dhal curry, chicken curry, or sambal. A staple breakfast in Kedah and all of Malaysia.

Normal Price: RM 1.50 - RM 4.00

Popular in: Kedah & Malaysia

Best Served With: Dhal curry or chicken curry

More Info

Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Egg
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Ghee or oil
