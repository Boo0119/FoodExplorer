Emping 米饼 ⭐⭐⭐⭐☆ (4.4/5)

Emping is a traditional Perlis snack made from rice flakes, usually deep-fried until crispy and lightly salted. It’s a popular light bite enjoyed with tea.

Normal Price: RM 3.00 - RM 6.00 per pack

Popular in: Perlis

Best Served With: Hot tea

More Info

Ingredients:

  • Rice flakes
  • Salt
  • Cooking oil
🔍

Kuih Dangai 椰丝糕 ⭐⭐⭐⭐☆ (4.5/5)

Kuih Dangai is a traditional coconut-based dessert, made from grated coconut mixed with glutinous rice flour and sugar, grilled until golden brown.

Normal Price: RM 2.00 - RM 5.00

Popular in: Perlis & Kedah

Best Served With: Hot coffee

More Info

Ingredients:

  • Grated coconut
  • Glutinous rice flour
  • Sugar
  • Pandan leaves (optional)
🔍

Pekasam Puyu 腌鱼 ⭐⭐⭐⭐☆ (4.6/5)

Pekasam Puyu is a preserved fish dish, where freshwater fish (ikan puyu) is fermented with rice, salt, and toasted rice grains, giving it a unique sour and savory taste.

Normal Price: RM 8.00 - RM 15.00 per pack

Popular in: Perlis & Northern Malaysia

Best Served With: Hot rice and sambal

More Info

Ingredients:

  • Freshwater fish (puyu)
  • Salt
  • Rice grains
  • Tamarind (optional)
🔍

Pulut Mempelam 芒果糯米饭 ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (4.8/5)

Pulut Mempelam is a sweet dessert consisting of glutinous rice served with ripe mango slices and creamy coconut milk, similar to Thai mango sticky rice but unique to Perlis.

Normal Price: RM 4.00 - RM 7.00

Popular in: Perlis

Best Served With: Extra coconut milk

More Info

Ingredients:

  • Glutinous rice
  • Fresh mango
  • Coconut milk
  • Palm sugar
  • Salt
